Unreal Memory

My sleeping brain, a generative machine.

At night my mind reates stories and colours and deeply emotive scenes. To the it, the spirits often whisper things the day is not ready to listen to.

Your visions are a source of insight and inspiration, but they hide behind a screen of forgetting. Each morning I cross the black mirror back to reality and leave them behind, lodged beyond my conscious mind. Each evening the vessel is filled, then emptied, stumbling on a delete key when returning. I wish to rise with recall of your stories, dream-image still imprinted in my eyelids.

To illuminate the interlude between night and day with the unreal memory of an unconscious dreamscape. And to bring this memory into reality, generating an artefact of visual poetry.

Project's transformation goals:

  1. To improve dream recall through systematic dream documentation.
  2. To learn how to create generative art pieces in a browser window with p5.js
    • Storing and manipulating data with .json files
    • Implementing processing sketches in a browser window.
    • Text analysis and generation with JavaScript.
    • Grabbing images from an API based on data.
  3. To study ideas and authors in relation to dream symbolism and the unconscious.
